My inspirations

I've read the works of others and they've played a pivotal part in spurring me to start this journey. Give them a look.

Models: Attract Women Through Honesty
Mark Manson's book is what I believe to be the best introduction to concepts of vulnerability, outcome independence, and lifestyle in regards to "the game".
Gorilla Mindset
Mike Cernovich's book on mindset, a concept that is so important to get right in undertaking any endeavour. The thoughts of an awkward-turned-social, boy-to-man.
The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck: A Counterintuitive Approach to Living a Good Life
Another book by Mark Manson, this time focused more on overall lifestyle - it's about living for yourself rather than what external forces think you should live for.
